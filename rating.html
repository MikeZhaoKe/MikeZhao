Zoomable Sunburst

Original Data: Tripadvisor: Top Attractions in Sydney

This radial space-filling visualization shows the cumulative values of Ratings & Reviews.

Key points about the diagram -

  • Arcs are clickable. Click a node to zoom in, or the center to zoom out.
  • There are three layers - Rating( 1.5-5.0 ), Category( Top25 ), Attractions in Sydney.
